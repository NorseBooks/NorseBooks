About NorseBooks

The NorseBooks project seeks to provide Luther College students a place to buy and sell textbooks to each other.

The selling process is simple enough. To sell a book, navigate to the new book page and fill out the form provided. You must list the name of the book and how much you want for it, among other things. Other students will be able to contact you via the in-app messaging system if they are interested in purchasing one of your books.

To purchase a book, navigate to the home page and search for the book you want. If you find a book you would like to purchase, you have the option to message the student who listed the book.

If you would like to give us feedback about the site, please see the feedback page.
